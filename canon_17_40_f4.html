We all know I love photography.

Landscape pictures are what I love, which means I primarily use wide angle lenses. The Canon 17-40mm is one of the best lenses that Canon makes for the price right now. At only $799.99, it covers a wide variety of focal lengths, which would allow me to basically use it as my primary lens. My other lens, the Canon 24-105mm f/4L would be able to cover the longer zoom lengths, which the 17-40mm would not. However, the widest angle of the 17-40mm would allow me to take very beautiful panoramic shots of the backyard sunset and other landscapes.

These are examples of what I can do already, just imagine what I could create with this lens!
