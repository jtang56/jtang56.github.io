I know, I know. Another lens???

I've dreamed about this lens for a while, and it's always been a dream for me. So why not make my dreams come true? The 50mm lens is often known as the "normal" lens, because its focal length is about equivalent to what you would see with the human eye. This lens is amazing for portrait shots, when you want crisp people and a blurry background.

The Canon 50mm f/1.2 has an aperture of 1.2, meaning it allows the most possible light through. It's perfect for dark situations and on a full frame body that I have (The Canon 6D, thank you all!) the pictures turn out amazing.
